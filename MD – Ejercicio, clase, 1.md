# MD – Ejercicio, clase, 1

1. $a_{n} = 6a_{n-1} - 8a_{n-2} + 3$

$$
x^{2} - 6x + 8 = 0 \to
\begin{cases}
r_{1} = 4 \\
r_{2} = 2
\end{cases} \\
$$

$$
H_{n} = A4^{n} + B2^{n} \\
\ \\
H_{0} = A4^{0} + B2^{0} = 1 \to A + B = 1 \\
H_{1} = A4^{1} + B2^{1} = 3 \to 4A + 2B = 3 \\
\ \\
\vdots \\
\space \\
\begin{cases}
A + B = 1 \\
4A + 2B = 3
\end{cases}
\begin{cases}
A = {1 \over 2}\\
B = {1 \over 2}
\end{cases} \\
\space \\
\vdots
\space \\
\space \\
a_{n} = 3 + {1 \over 2}(4^{n} + 2^{n})
$$
